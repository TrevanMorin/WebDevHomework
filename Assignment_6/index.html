Description

You can watch the movies starting from Episode IV and proceed in release order or watch them starting from Episode I and go in chronological order. Both approaches are great. Keep in mind that some viewers and I myself have had mixed opinions about the sequel trilogy.I have great disdain for the sequel trilogy and would not recommend it. If you were to watch it I’d recommend it to be watch last cause it is last in the timeline and you will have a lot more perspective on why it’s problematic with the knowledge of all the previous content. There are some caveats such as to know what’s going on in Mandalorian Season 3 you need to watch book of Boba Fett season one first. I recommend watching the movies and shows in the order specified above for the most context and enjoyment. There is also far more content to explore in the forms of books and videogames. Star Wars is a Great story that’s fun to get into and I hope you enjoy it too.
