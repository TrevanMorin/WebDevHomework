Comic Book Universe

The Boys

"The Boys" is a comic series created by writer Garth Ennis and artist Darick Robertson. First published by Wildstorm before moving to Dynamite Entertainment, "The Boys" offers a dark and satirical take on the superhero genre. The series explores the consequences of superheroes' unchecked power. Its success led to the creation of a hit TV series on Amazon Prime, gaining a massive fanbase. "The Boys" has become a cultural phenomenon, sparking discussions about the morality and influence of superheroes in modern storytelling.
