Comic Book Universe

Marvel & DC

Marvel and DC Comics are two giants in the comic book industry, each with a storied history of creating iconic characters and compelling stories. Marvel, founded in 1939, and DC, with roots tracing back to 1934, have shaped the superhero genre. Both publishers have produced legendary superheroes like Spider-Man, Iron Man, Superman, and Batman. The success of their comics has translated into a multitude of blockbuster films, TV series, and video games, creating a vast and enduring legacy in popular culture.
