Comic Book Universe

Marvel & DC

Marvel and DC Comics, two juggernauts in the comic book industry, have shaped the superhero genre since their founding in 1939 (Marvel) and 1934 (DC). These publishers have created iconic characters such as Spider-Man, Iron Man, Superman, and Batman, contributing to the global popularity of superheroes. Both Marvel and DC expanded into various media, including blockbuster films, television series, animated shows, and video games. The enduring success of these franchises has solidified their place in pop culture, with a constant stream of new adaptations captivating audiences worldwide.
