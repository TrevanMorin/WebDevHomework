Comic Book Universe

Invincible

"Invincible" is a superhero saga co-created by Robert Kirkman, Cory Walker, and Ryan Ottley. The comic explores the challenges faced by Mark Grayson, a teenager bestowed with superpowers. The narrative is rich and intricate, offering readers a compelling superhero experience. The success of the comic translated into an animated TV series, bringing the characters to life. "Invincible" has garnered a dedicated fanbase and acclaim for its fresh take on the superhero genre. The adaptation has further expanded the reach of this beloved comic.
