Comic Book Universe

The Walking Dead

"The Walking Dead" comic, created by Robert Kirkman, follows a group of survivors in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by zombies. Launched in 2003, the series became a massive success, achieving critical acclaim and building a dedicated fanbase. Its impact extended beyond comics, inspiring a hit TV series that premiered in 2010, with numerous seasons and spin-offs. The show's popularity brought the zombie genre to new heights, and "The Walking Dead" expanded into video games, merchandise, and more.
