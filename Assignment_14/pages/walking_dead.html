Comic Book Universe

The Walking Dead

"The Walking Dead" is a groundbreaking comic series by Robert Kirkman, Tony Moore, and Charlie Adlard. It delves into a post-apocalyptic world overrun by zombies, focusing on the survival of a group of diverse characters. The success of the comic led to the creation of a massively popular TV series with the same name. The show has achieved widespread acclaim, drawing a massive global following over its multiple seasons. Additionally, "The Walking Dead" expanded into video games, giving fans interactive experiences in the same universe.
