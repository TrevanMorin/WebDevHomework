Welcome to the Beersbee Guide!

Learn how to play Beersbee, a fun and social outdoor game.

Setup

Set up the Beersbee game with these simple steps:

  • Place two PVC poles 30 feet apart.
  • Cut PVC pipes to use as poles and hammer them into the ground until around 5-5.5 feet tall.
  • Use 2 strong plastic frisbees.
  • Place a beer bottle on top of each pole.

Rules

Play the game with the following rules:

  • Teams can have one or two players.
  • Players take alternating turns.
  • Both players stand behind the same pole.
  • Players throw frisbees at the opponents' pole.
  • Score points by knocking down the opponent's bottle or making your opponent drop frisbees.
  • Scoring: Dropped frisbees - 1 point, Frisbee hits pole and bottle hits ground - 2 points, Direct hit on the bottle - 3 points.
  • First to 21 points wins.

Scoring

Score points in the Beersbee game:

  • Hit your opponent's pole or target.
  • Score three points if you knock the bottle to the ground and it breaks.

Defense

Defend your position with these rules:

  • Players must remain behind the poles at all times.
  • Your hand can't move in front of the pole.
  • Hold a beverage in your hand at all times.
