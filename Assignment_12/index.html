Welcome to the Beersbee Guide!

Learn how to play Beersbee, a fun and social outdoor game.

Scoring

Score points by hitting your opponent's pole or target. Score three points if you knock the bottle to the ground and it breaks.

Defense

Players must remain behind the poles at all times. Your hand can't move in front of the pole. Hold a beverage in your hand at all times.
