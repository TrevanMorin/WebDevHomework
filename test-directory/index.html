So heres a summary of Star Wars

Episode I: The Phantom Menace (1999) "The Phantom Menace" transports us to a galaxy far, far away, before the events of the original trilogy. The Galactic Republic is in turmoil, and dark forces are at play. Jedi Knights Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi stumble upon a young slave named Anakin Skywalker on the desert planet Tatooine. Anakin's extraordinary Force abilities hint at his destiny. Meanwhile, the Sith Lord Darth Sidious plots to destabilize the Republic. The film introduces the menacing Sith apprentice, Darth Maul, who engages in a lightsaber duel with the Jedi. As the movie concludes, Anakin begins his Jedi training, setting the stage for his pivotal role in the galaxy's future. Episode II: Attack of the Clones (2002) "Attack of the Clones" takes place a decade after Episode I. Anakin Skywalker, now a Jedi apprentice, is assigned to protect Senator Padmé Amidala, who faces assassination attempts. Anakin and Padmé fall in love, forbidden by the Jedi Code. Simultaneously, a clone army is secretly commissioned for the Republic, leading to the onset of the Clone Wars. The movie unveils a conspiracy orchestrated by Darth Sidious and culminates in a massive battle on the planet Geonosis. As the Jedi fight for their lives, it becomes evident that the Sith have returned, setting the stage for the galaxy's descent into chaos. Episode III: Revenge of the Sith (2005) "Revenge of the Sith" is a heart-wrenching tale of Anakin Skywalker's fall to the dark side and his transformation into Darth Vader. The Clone Wars rage on, while Chancellor Palpatine, secretly Darth Sidious, manipulates Anakin to become his Sith apprentice. Anakin ultimately betrays the Jedi Order, leading to a climactic lightsaber duel with his former mentor, Obi-Wan Kenobi, on the volcanic planet Mustafar. Anakin is gravely injured and left for dead. Meanwhile, Sidious proclaims the establishment of the Galactic Empire. The film ends with the birth of Luke and Leia Skywalker, hidden from their fallen father. Episode IV: A New Hope (1977) "A New Hope" transports us to a galaxy oppressed by the Galactic Empire. Luke Skywalker, a young farm boy, discovers his Force sensitivity and joins forces with Princess Leia, Han Solo, and Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi to combat the Empire's tyranny. The Rebel Alliance hatches a daring plan to destroy the Empire's superweapon, the Death Star. Luke's journey as a Jedi begins as he confronts the dark and powerful Darth Vader. The Rebels succeed in destroying the Death Star, providing a glimmer of hope to the oppressed galaxy. Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back (1980) "The Empire Strikes Back" continues the Rebel Alliance's struggle against the relentless Empire. Luke, Han, and Leia face personal trials and tribulations. Luke receives advanced Jedi training from the wise Yoda while the Rebel Alliance comes under relentless assault. The film reaches its climax with a lightsaber duel between Luke and Darth Vader, leading to the shocking revelation that Vader is Luke's father. Our heroes are separated, with Han Solo frozen in carbonite, leaving the galaxy in a state of uncertainty and despair. Episode VI: Return of the Jedi (1983) "Return of the Jedi" brings the epic saga to its thrilling conclusion. Luke Skywalker embarks on a mission to redeem his father, Darth Vader, from the clutches of the dark side. The Rebel Alliance plans to destroy the second Death Star while rescuing Han Solo from the vile gangster Jabba the Hutt. A climactic battle ensues, with Luke confronting the Emperor himself. In a moment of ultimate redemption, Darth Vader turns on the Emperor to save his son, ultimately sacrificing himself in the process. The Death Star is destroyed, and the galaxy is liberated from the Empire's oppressive rule. The film closes with the spirits of Anakin Skywalker, Obi-Wan Kenobi, and Yoda appearing as Force ghosts, symbolizing the triumph of good over evil.
